12. Facing the south, they shall pour out water with both hands on (those days of the period of impurity) which are marked by odd numbers. [^9] 


[^9]:  Viṣṇu XIX, 7; Gautama XIV, 40. 'On those days of the period of impurity which are marked by odd numbers,' i.e. 'on the first, third, fifth, seventh, and ninth, as has been declared by Gautama.'--Kṛṣṇapaṇḍita.
