<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

गर्भा वा एत उक्थानां यन्निविदः ।
</details

<details><summary>English</summary>

*On the Proper Place of the Nivids in the Three Libations.*

The Nivids are the embryos of the Śastras (*ukthas*).
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

गर्भा वा एत उक्थानां यन्निविदः ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

तद्यत्पुरस्तादुक्थानां प्रातःसवने धीयन्ते तस्मात्पराञ्चो गर्भा धीयन्ते पराञ्चः सम्भवन्ति ।
</details

<details><summary>English</summary>

At the morning libation, they are put before the Śastras (*ukthas*), because the embryos are lying in the womb with their heads turned downward, and thus they are born (the head coming first out of the womb).
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

तद्यत्पुरस्तादुक्थानां प्रातःसवने धीयन्ते तस्मात्पराञ्चो गर्भा धीयन्ते पराञ्चः सम्भवन्ति ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

यन्मध्यतो मध्यंदिने धीयन्ते तस्मान्मध्ये गर्भा धृताः ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

At the midday libation, the Nivids are put in the midst (of the Śastras). This is done because the embryos have their hold in the middle of the womb.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

यन्मध्यतो मध्यंदिने धीयन्ते तस्मान्मध्ये गर्भा धृताः ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

यदन्ततस्तृतीयसवने धीयन्ते तस्मादमुतो ऽर्वाञ्चो गर्भाः प्रजायन्ते प्रजात्यै ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

At the evening libation, the Nivids are repeated at the end (of the Śastras), because the embryos are coming down from thence (the womb) when they are brought forth.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

यदन्ततस्तृतीयसवने धीयन्ते तस्मादमुतो ऽर्वाञ्चो गर्भाः प्रजायन्ते प्रजात्यै ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

प्रजायते प्रजया पशुभिर्य एवं वेद ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

He who has such a knowledge is blessed with children and cattle.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

प्रजायते प्रजया पशुभिर्य एवं वेद ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

पेशा वा एत उक्थानां यन्निविदः ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

The Nivids are the decorations of the Śastras.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पेशा वा एत उक्थानां यन्निविदः ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

तद्यत्पुरस्तादुक्थानां प्रातःसवने धीयन्ते यथैव प्रवयणतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

They are put, at the morning libation, before the Śastras, just as a weaver weaves decorations in the beginning of a cloth.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

तद्यत्पुरस्तादुक्थानां प्रातःसवने धीयन्ते यथैव प्रवयणतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

यन्मध्यतो मध्यंदिने धीयन्ते यथैव मध्यतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तद्यदन्ततस्तृतीयसवने धीयन्ते यथैवावप्रज्जनतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

At the midday libation, they are put in the midst (of the Śastras), just as a weaver weaves decorations in the midst (of a cloth).
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

यन्मध्यतो मध्यंदिने धीयन्ते यथैव मध्यतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

यदन्ततस्तृतीयसवने धीयन्ते यथैवावप्रज्जनतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

At the evening libation, they are put at the end (of the Śastras), just as the weaver weaves decorations in the end of a cloth (*avaprajjana*).
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

यदन्ततस्तृतीयसवने धीयन्ते यथैवावप्रज्जनतः पेशः कुर्यात्तादृक्तत् ।
</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

सर्वतो यज्ञस्य पेशसा शोभते य एवं वेद ।
</details>

<details><summary>English</summary>

He who has such a knowledge is ornamented on all parts with the decoration of the sacrifice.
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

सर्वतो यज्ञस्य पेशसा शोभते य एवं वेद ।
</details>
