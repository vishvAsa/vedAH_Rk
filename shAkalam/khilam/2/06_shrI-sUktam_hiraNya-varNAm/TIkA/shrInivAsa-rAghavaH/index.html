SRI SUKTA.

[Sri Sukta is a hymn of fifteen verses in praise of the Goddess Sri or Lakshmi and is a тАШKhila RikтАЩ (supplemental hymn) of the Rig Veda. The first two are addressed to Jatavedah (Narayana) and request him to bring Sri to the devotee. The third and the fourth are invocations to Sri herself and by the fifth, the devotee does the тАШPrapatti.тАЩ By the next five, he prays for the removal of all evil and ignorance, and for the gift of learning and wealth and fame. The next two request the father and attendant of Sri to make Her dwell in his house. The last three are, as in the opening verses, addressed to Jatavedah requesting him to bring Lakshmi to him.]
