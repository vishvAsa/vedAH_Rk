In virtual mirror-image to the previous hymn, this one begins with a single Dawn, but turns for some verses (2тАУ4) to the plural Dawns, before returning to a single Dawn in the final two verses (5тАУ6). The structure of this hymn is more cunning than the somewhat bland VI.64, however.
