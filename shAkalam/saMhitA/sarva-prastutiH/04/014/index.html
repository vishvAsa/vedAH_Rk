Like IV.13 this is a dawn hymn, and, as was noted there, it is closely paired with that hymn. The chief divinities are the same, mentioned in more or less the same order: Agni, Dawn, the A┼Ыvins, Savitar, and the Sun; the final verses are identical; and the first two verses are stylistic variants of each other. In verse 1 of both hymns Agni looks toward the Dawns, and the A┼Ыvins [/N─Бsatyas] are urged to come to the sacrifice; in verse 2 Savitar braces his beacon, and the Sun rises.
