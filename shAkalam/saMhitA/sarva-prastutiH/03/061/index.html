The only hymn dedicated to Dawn in Maс╣Зс╕Нala III, it combines the usual themes: the beauty of the young goddess, her relation to the morning ritual and the ritual fire, and her role as giver of rich goods because of the distribution of priestly gifts at the Morning Pressing.
