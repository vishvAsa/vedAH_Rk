Jamison Brereton Notes

This vs. sits somewhat oddly in a soma hymn, and its subject, and indeed its general aim, are not clear until the 3rd p─Бda.

It begins with a fem. instr. demonst. ay─Б╠Бwithout expressed referent, and as Renou points out, a number of fem. referents are possible. However, the verb that begins c, st├бvai тАШI will praiseтАЩ, makes dh─л╠Б- тАШinsightful thoughtтАЩ (or some other reference to a verbal product) quite likely, and the phrase ay─Б╠Бdhiy─Б╠Бis in fact fairly common (I.166.13, V.45.11 [2x] [in the hymn adjacent to Avats─БraтАЩs V.44 though V.45 is not attributed to him], VI.71.6, VIII.13.8, 93.17).
