वास्तोष्पतीये कर्मणि १
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

वास्तोष्पतीये कर्मणि १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1 [^1] . At the sacrifice to Vāstoṣpati - 

[^1]:  4, 1. The sacrifice to Vāstoṣpati is celebrated when the sacrificer enters his new house.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अग्निं दधामि मनसा शिवेनायमस्तु सङ्गमनो वसूनाम् । मा नो हिँ सी स्थविरं मा कुमारं शं नो भव द्विपदे शं चतुष्पद इति गृह्यमग्निं बाह्यत उपसमाधाय २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अग्निं दधामि मनसा शिवेनायमस्तु सङ्गमनो वसूनाम् । मा नो हिँ सी स्थविरं मा कुमारं शं नो भव द्विपदे शं चतुष्पद इति गृह्यमग्निं बाह्यत उपसमाधाय २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2 [^2] . Having established the (sacred) domestic fire outside with (the words), 'I place (here) Agni with genial mind; may he be the assembler of goods. Do no harm to us, to the old nor to the young; be a saviour to us, to men and animals!' - 

[^2]:  Comp. above, I, 7, 9.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

प्रागग्रेषु नवेषु कुशेषूदकुम्भं नवं प्रतिष्ठाप्य
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

प्रागग्रेषु नवेषु कुशेषूदकुम्भं नवं प्रतिष्ठाप्य
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. Having put a new water-pot on fresh eastward-pointed Kuśa-grass,

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अरिष्टा अस्माकं वीरा मा परासेचि नो धनमित्यभिमन्त्र्य ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अरिष्टा अस्माकं वीरा मा परासेचि नो धनमित्यभिमन्त्र्य ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. And spoken over it (the words), 'Unhurt be our men, may our riches not be squandered!' - 

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

रथन्तरस्य स्तोत्रियेण पुनरादायं ककुप्कारं तिस्रः पूर्वाह्णे जुहोति ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

रथन्तरस्य स्तोत्रियेण पुनरादायं ककुप्कारं तिस्रः पूर्वाह्णे जुहोति ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5 [^3] . He sacrifices three oblations in the forenoon with the Stotriya text of the Rathantara with repetition and Kakubh-forming;

[^3]:  On the way of reciting a Pragātha, so as to form three verses, see Indische Studien, VIII, 25; Zeitschrift der deutschen Morg. Gesellschaft, XXXVIII, 476. The Stotriya of the Rathantara is Rig-veda VII, 32, 22 seq.; that of the Vāmadevya, IV, 31, 1-3; that of the Bṛhat, VI, 46, 1 seq.

</details>

वामदेव्यस्य मध्यन्दिने ६
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

वामदेव्यस्य मध्यन्दिने ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. (Three oblations with the Stotriya) of the Vāmadevya at midday;

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

बृहतोऽपराह्णे ७
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

बृहतोऽपराह्णे ७
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

7. Of the Bṛhat in the afternoon;

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

महाव्याहृतयश्चतस्रो वास्तोष्पत इति तिस्रोऽमीवहा वास्तोष्पते वास्तोष्पते ध्रुवा स्थूणा सौविष्टकृती दशमी स्थालीपाकस्य रात्रौ ८
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

महाव्याहृतयश्चतस्रो वास्तोष्पत इति तिस्रोऽमीवहा वास्तोष्पते वास्तोष्पते ध्रुवा स्थूणा सौविष्टकृती दशमी स्थालीपाकस्य रात्रौ ८
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

8. The four Mahāvyāhṛtis, the three verses, 'Vāstoṣpati!' (Rig-veda VII, 54, 1-3), (the single verses,) 'Driving away calamity,' (and) 'Vāstoṣpati, a firm post' (Rig-veda VII, 55, 1; VIII, 17, 14), and to (Agni) Sviṣṭakṛt a tenth oblation of cooked food at night.

</details>

ज्येष्ठं पुत्रमादाय जायां च सहधान्यः प्रपद्येत ९
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

ज्येष्ठं पुत्रमादाय जायां च सहधान्यः प्रपद्येत ९
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

9. Taking with himself his eldest son and his wife, carrying grain, let him enter (the house with the words),

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

इन्द्रस्य गृहाः शिवा वसुमन्तो वरूथिनस्तानहं प्रपद्ये सह जायया सह प्रजया सह पशुभिः सह रायस्पोषेण सह यन्मे किञ्चास्ति तेन १०
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

इन्द्रस्य गृहाः शिवा वसुमन्तो वरूथिनस्तानहं प्रपद्ये सह जायया सह प्रजया सह पशुभिः सह रायस्पोषेण सह यन्मे किञ्चास्ति तेन १०
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

'Indra's house is blessed, wealthy, protecting; that I enter with my wife, with offspring, with cattle, with increase of wealth, with everything that is mine.'

</details>
