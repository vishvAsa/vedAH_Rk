<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

उत्तमायामपूपाञ्जुहोत्य्
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

उत्तमायामपूपाञ्जुहोत्य् 
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. On the last (Aṣṭakā) he sacrifices cakes,

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

उक्थ्यश्चातिरात्रश्च सद्यःक्रीश्छन्दसा सह । अपूपकृदष्टके नमस्ते सुमनामुखि स्वाहेति २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

उक्थ्यश्चातिरात्रश्च सद्यःक्रीश्छन्दसा सह । अपूपकृदष्टके नमस्ते सुमनामुखि स्वाहेति २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. With the words, 'The Ukthya and the Atirātra. the Sadyaḥkrī together with the metre - Aṣṭakā!

Preparer of cakes! Adoration to thee, O glad-faced one. Svāhā!

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

गोपशुरजपशु स्थालीपाको वापि
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

गोपशुरजपशु स्थालीपाको वापि
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3 [^1] . A cow or a goat is the animal (to be sacrificed), or a mess of cooked food (should be offered).

[^1]:  14, 3-6. This is one of the passages which the author has taken unchanged from a more ancient Sūtra; see Āśv. II, 4, 8-11; Gobhila IV, 1 (end of the chapter). The Sūtras do not refer, as their position would seem to indicate, to the third, but to the second Aṣṭakā.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

वा गोग्रासमाहरेद् ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

वा गोग्रासमाहरेद् ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. Or he may optionally offer food to a cow.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अपि वारण्ये कक्षमपादहेदेषा मेऽष्टकेति ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अपि वारण्ये कक्षमपादहेदेषा मेऽष्टकेति ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5 [^2] . Or he may optionally burn down brushwood in the forest and say, 'This is my Aṣṭakā.'

[^2]:  Comp. Weber, loc. cit., p. 342, note 1.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

न त्वेव न कुर्वीत न त्वेव न कुर्वीत ६
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

न त्वेव न कुर्वीत न त्वेव न कुर्वीत ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. But let him not neglect to do (one of these things). But let him not neglect to do (one of these things).

Here ends the Third Adhyāya.

</details>
