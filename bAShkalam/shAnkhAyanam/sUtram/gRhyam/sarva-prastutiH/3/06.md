<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अनाहिताग्निः प्रवत्स्यन्गृहान्समीक्षते १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. One who has not set up the (sacred Śrauta) fires, when setting out on a journey, looks at his house.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

इमान्मे मित्रावरुणौ गृहान्गोपायतं युवम् अविनष्टानविह्रुतान्पूषैनानभिरक्षत्वास्माकं पुनरागमात् २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

इमान्मे मित्रावरुणौ गृहान्गोपायतं युवम् अविनष्टानविह्रुतान्पूषैनानभिरक्षत्वास्माकं पुनरागमात् २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. (He murmurs the text,) 'Do ye both, Mitra and Varuṇa, protect this house for me; unscathed, undisturbed, may Pūṣan guard it till our return;'

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अपि पन्थामगन्महीति च जपति ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अपि पन्थामगन्महीति च जपति ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. And murmurs (the verse), 'Upon the path we have entered' (Rig-veda VI, 51, 16).

</details>
