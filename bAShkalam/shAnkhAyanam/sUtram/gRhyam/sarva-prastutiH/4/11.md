</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. Nor (look) at the sun while it rises or sets,

</details>

अनाप्तम् ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अनाप्तम् ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. At an enemy,

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अकार्यकारिणं ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अकार्यकारिणं ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. At an evil-doer,

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

प्रेतस्पर्शिनं ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

प्रेतस्पर्शिनं ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5. At a person that has to touch dead bodies.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

सूतिकोदक्याभ्यां न संवदेद् ६
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

सूतिकोदक्याभ्यां न संवदेद् ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. Let him not talk with a woman who has recently been confined or who has her courses,

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

एतैश्च
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

एतैश्च

</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

7 [^2] . Nor with those (mentioned before).

[^2]:  Etaiḥ pūrvoktaiḥ anāptādibhir na saṃvadet. Nārāyaṇa.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

उद्धृततेजाँसि न भुञ्जीत ८
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

उद्धृततेजाँसि न भुञ्जीत ८
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

8. Let him not eat food from which its strength is taken away.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

न यातयामैः कार्यं कुर्यान् ९
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

न यातयामैः कार्यं कुर्यान् ९
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

9. Let him not do his work with implements wasted by use.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

न सह भुञ्जीत १०
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

न सह भुञ्जीत १०
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

10 [^3] . Let him not eat together (with his wife),

[^3]:  Nārāyaṇa states that 'with his wife' is to be supplied to this Sūtra, which indeed is rendered probable through the comparison of Gautama IX, 32; Manu IV, 43, &c.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

न शेषं ११
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

न शेषं ११
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

11 [^4] . Nor remnants (of food).

[^4]:  Here also Nārāyaṇa understands bhāryāyā bhuktaśeṣam.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पितृदेवतातिथिभृत्यानां शेषं भुञ्जीत
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

12. Remnants of (food belonging to the) Manes, gods, guests, and servants he may eat.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

उञ्छशिलमयाचितप्रतिग्रहः साधुभ्यो याचितो वा याजनं वृत्तिः १३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

उञ्छशिलमयाचितप्रतिग्रहः साधुभ्यो याचितो वा याजनं वृत्तिः १३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

13. Gleaning ears of corn, receiving alms unasked for, or for which he has asked the good, performing sacrifices for others, are the means of livelihood;

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

पूर्वम्पूर्वं गरीयो
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पूर्वम्पूर्वं गरीयो
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

14. (Of these) each preceding one is the more respectable.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

असंसिध्यमानायां वैश्यवृत्तिर्वा
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

असंसिध्यमानायां वैश्यवृत्तिर्वा

</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

15 [^5] . Or if (his livelihood) cannot be gained (in one of the ways mentioned), let him follow the occupation of a Vaiśya.

[^5]:  Comp. Professor Bühler's note on Gautama X, 5, S.B.E., vol. ii, p. 225.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अप्रमत्तः पितृदैवतकार्येष्व् 
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

16. (He shall be) careful about his duties towards Manes and gods.

</details>

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

ऋतौ स्वदारगामी १७
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

17. In due time (he shall) have intercourse with his wife.

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

18. He shall not lie down (to sleep) in the day-time,

</details>

19 [^6] . Nor during the first or the last watch of the night.

[^6]:  Rātreḥ pūrvaprahare rātreḥ paścimaprahare ca. Nārāyaṇa.

</details>

</details>

</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>
