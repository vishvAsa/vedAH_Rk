<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

रोहिण्यां कृषिकर्माणि कारयेत् १
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

रोहिण्यां कृषिकर्माणि कारयेत् १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. Under (the Nakṣatra) Rohiṇī he shall have the ploughing done.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

पुरस्तात्कर्मणां प्राच्यां क्षेत्रमर्यादायां द्यावापृथिवीबलिं हरेद् २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पुरस्तात्कर्मणां प्राच्यां क्षेत्रमर्यादायां द्यावापृथिवीबलिं हरेद् २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. Before it is done, he shall offer at the eastern boundary of his field a Bali to Heaven and Earth.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

द्यावापृथिवीययर्चा नमो द्यावापृथिवीभ्यामिति चोपस्थानं ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

द्यावापृथिवीययर्चा नमो द्यावापृथिवीभ्यामिति चोपस्थानं ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. With a verse sacred to Heaven and Earth and with the words, 'Adoration to Heaven and Earth!' (he performs his) worship (to Heaven and Earth).

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

प्रथमप्रयोगे सीरस्य ब्राह्मणः सीरं स्पृशेच्छुनं नः फाला इत्येतामनुब्रुवन् ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

प्रथमप्रयोगे सीरस्य ब्राह्मणः सीरं स्पृशेच्छुनं नः फाला इत्येतामनुब्रुवन् ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. When the plough is being put into motion first, let a Brāhmaṇa touch the plough reciting this (verse), 'For luck may us the plough-shares' (Rig-veda IV, 57, 8).

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

क्षेत्रस्य पतिनेति प्रदक्षिणं प्रत्यृचं प्रतिदिशमुपस्थानम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

क्षेत्रस्य पतिनेति प्रदक्षिणं प्रत्यृचं प्रतिदिशमुपस्थानम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5. 'Through the lord of the field' - with (this hymn) (Rig-veda IV, 57), verse by verse, to the different directions (of the sky), from left to right, worship is done.

</details>
