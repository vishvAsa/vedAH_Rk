<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

मधुपर्के च सोमे च पितृदैवतकर्मणि अत्रैव पशवो हिँ स्या नान्यत्रेत्यब्रवीन्मनुः १
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

मधुपर्के च सोमे च पितृदैवतकर्मणि अत्रैव पशवो हिँ स्या नान्यत्रेत्यब्रवीन्मनुः १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. 'At the Madhuparka and at the Soma sacrifice, at the sacred rites for fathers (Manes) and gods only animals may be killed, not elsewhere: thus has Manu said.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

आचार्यश्च पिता चोभौ सखा चानतिथिर्गृहे ते यद्विदध्युस्तत्कुर्यादिति धर्मो विधीयते २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

आचार्यश्च पिता चोभौ सखा चानतिथिर्गृहे ते यद्विदध्युस्तत्कुर्यादिति धर्मो विधीयते २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. 'Both his teacher and his father, and also a friend who does not stay in his house as a guest: whatever these dispose, that let him do; such is the established custom.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

नैकग्रामीणमतिथिं विप्रोष्यागतमेव च उपस्थितं गृहे विद्याद्भार्या यत्राग्नयोऽपि वा ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

नैकग्रामीणमतिथिं विप्रोष्यागतमेव च उपस्थितं गृहे विद्याद्भार्या यत्राग्नयोऽपि वा ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3 [^1] . 'Let him not consider as a guest a person living in the same village, or one that comes in returning from a journey; (but let him consider as a guest only) one who has arrived at his house where the wife or the fires (of the host) are.

[^1]:  16, 3. Comp. Gautama V, 40, &c.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अग्निहोत्रं बलिवर्दाः काले चातिथिरागतः बालाश्च कुलवृद्धाश्च निर्दहन्त्यपमानिताः ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अग्निहोत्रं बलिवर्दाः काले चातिथिरागतः बालाश्च कुलवृद्धाश्च निर्दहन्त्यपमानिताः ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4 [^2] . '(The fire of) the Agnihotra, bulls, and a guest that has come in at the right time, children and persons of noble families: these burn up him who neglects them.

[^2]:  On the right time for the arrival of a guest, see Gautama, loc. cit.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अनड्वानग्निहोत्रं च ब्रह्मचारी च ते त्रयः अश्नन्त एव सिध्यन्ति नैषां सिद्धिरनश्नताम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अनड्वानग्निहोत्रं च ब्रह्मचारी च ते त्रयः अश्नन्त एव सिध्यन्ति नैषां सिद्धिरनश्नताम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5. 'A bull, the Agnihotra, and a student, these three prosper only if they eat; there is no prosperity for them, if they do not eat.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

देवताः पुरुषं गृह्या अहरहर्गृहमेधिनम् भागार्थमुपसर्पन्ति ताभ्यो निर्वप्तुमर्हति ६
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

देवताः पुरुषं गृह्या अहरहर्गृहमेधिनम् भागार्थमुपसर्पन्ति ताभ्यो निर्वप्तुमर्हति ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. 'Day by day the domestic deities approach the man who performs the domestic rites, in order to receive their share; (that) let him pour out to them.

</details>
