<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

चतुर्थे मासि गर्भरक्षणं १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. In the fourth month the Garbharakṣaṇa (i.e. the ceremony for the protection of the embryo),

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

ब्रह्मणाग्निः संविदान इति षट् स्थालीपाकस्य हुत्वा
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

ब्रह्मणाग्निः संविदान इति षट् स्थालीपाकस्य हुत्वा 

</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2. Sacrificing six oblations from a mess of cooked food with (the six verses of the hymn), 'Agni, joined with the prayer' (Rig-veda X, 162),

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अक्षीभ्यां ते नासिकाभ्यामिति प्रत्यृचमाज्यलेपेनाङ्गान्यनुविमृज्य ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अक्षीभ्यां ते नासिकाभ्यामिति प्रत्यृचमाज्यलेपेनाङ्गान्यनुविमृज्य ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. With (the verses), 'From thy eyes, thy nose' (Rig-veda X, 163), verse by verse besmearing her limbs with Ājya salve.

</details>
