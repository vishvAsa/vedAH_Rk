<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

दधिक्राव्णो अकारिषमिति दधि सम्पिबेयातां १
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

दधिक्राव्णो अकारिषमिति दधि सम्पिबेयातां १
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

1. With the verse, 'I praised Dadhikrāvan' (Rig-veda IV, 39, 6), let them drink together curds.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

वाग्यतावासीयातामा ध्रुवदर्शनाद् २
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

वाग्यतावासीयातामा ध्रुवदर्शनाद् २
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

2 [^1] . Let them sit silent, when the sun has set, until the polar-star appears.

[^1]:  17, 2, 3. I have changed in the translation the division of these Sūtras; the native authorities divide after dhruvadarśanāt, while I propose to divide after astamite.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अस्तमिते ध्रुवं दर्शयति ध्रुवैधि पोष्या मयीति ३
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अस्तमिते ध्रुवं दर्शयति ध्रुवैधि पोष्या मयीति ३
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

3. He shows her the polar-star with the words, 'Firm be thou, thriving with me!'

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

ध्रुवं पश्यामि प्रजां विन्देयेति ब्रूयात् ४
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

ध्रुवं पश्यामि प्रजां विन्देयेति ब्रूयात् ४
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

4. Let her say, 'I see the polar-star; may I obtain offspring.'

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

त्रिरात्रं ब्रह्मचर्यं चरेयाताम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

त्रिरात्रं ब्रह्मचर्यं चरेयाताम् ५
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

5. Through a period of three nights let them refrain from conjugal intercourse.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

अधः शयीयातां ६
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

अधः शयीयातां ६
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

6. Let them sleep on the ground.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

दध्योदनं सम्भुञ्जीयातां पिबतं च तृप्णुतं चेति तृचेन ७
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

दध्योदनं सम्भुञ्जीयातां पिबतं च तृप्णुतं चेति तृचेन ७
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

7. Let them eat together boiled rice with curds, with the three verses, 'Drink and satiate yourselves' (Rig-veda VIII, 35, 10).

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

सायम्प्रातर्वैवाह्यमग्निं परिचरेयातामग्नये स्वाहाग्नये स्विष्टकृते स्वाहेति ८
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

सायम्प्रातर्वैवाह्यमग्निं परिचरेयातामग्नये स्वाहाग्नये स्विष्टकृते स्वाहेति ८
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

8. Let them serve the nuptial fire in the evening and in the morning with the words, 'To Agni svāhā! To Agni Sviṣṭakṛt svāhā!'

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

पुमाँ सौ मित्रावरुणौ पुमाँ सावश्विनावुभौ । पुमानिन्द्र श्चाग्निश्च पुमाँ सं वर्धतां मयि स्वाहेति पूर्वां गर्भकामा ९
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

पुमाँ सौ मित्रावरुणौ पुमाँ सावश्विनावुभौ । पुमानिन्द्र श्चाग्निश्च पुमाँ सं वर्धतां मयि स्वाहेति पूर्वां गर्भकामा ९
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

9. 'Let the two men Mitra and Varuṇa, let the two men, the Aśvins both, let the man Indra and also Agni make a man grow in me. Svāhā!' - with (these words she offers) the first oblation if she is desirous of pregnancy.

</details>

<details open><summary>विश्वास-प्रस्तुतिः</summary>

दशरात्रमविप्रवासः १०
</details>

<details><summary>मूलम्</summary>

दशरात्रमविप्रवासः १०
</details>

<details><summary>Oldenberg</summary>

10. For ten days they are not to set out (from home).

</details>
